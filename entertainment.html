Things to do on Taniti

Beaches

The beaches on Taniti are beautiful and relaxing. The water is warm and clear.

Beach Name Location Hours
North Beach North beach road 6:00 AM - 8:00 PM
South Beach South shore drive 6:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Rainforest

The rainforest is a great place to explore. There are many trails to hike, zip-lines to ride, and wildlife to see.

Trail Name Location Hours
Birders Paradise 15 Parakeet Drive 6:00 AM - Dusk
Jungle Walk 27 Jungle Ave 6:00 AM - Dusk
Zip-line in the Jungle 17 Jungle Ave 9:00 AM - Dusk

There are many attractions in the city of Taniti at Merriton Landing. Some of the most popular include:

Guided Tours

Guided tours are available for the rainforest and the city. Tours are available in English, Spanish, and French. Tours are available for groups of 2-10 people. Tours are available for the following:

Tour Name Location Hours
City Walking Tour City Park 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM
Rainforest Hike 15 Parakeet Drive 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM
City BusTour 5 Dance Street 8:00 PM - 12:00 AM
Taniti Volcano Adventure 1 Volcano Road 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
