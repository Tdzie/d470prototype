Eating on the island

Grocery Stores:

Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.

Store Type Store Name Hours
Supermarket Island Mart 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM
Supermarket Tropical Grocer 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM
Smaller Grocery Store Palm Tree Foods 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
Smaller Grocery Store Bayview Groceries 7:30 AM - 8:30 PM
Convenience Store QuickStop 24 Hours

Dining-out on the Island:

From a quick bite to fine dining, you will find it all on the island.

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine.
